INSTRUCTIONS

OBJECTIVE:

The objective is to reach 100 points first.

NUMBER OF PLAYERS:

2

RULES:

1) The player who reaches 100 points first will win.
2) When player rolls the dice, the dice number will be added to current score.
3) Player can either 'hold' the current score and added it to the final score or he/she can continue rolling the dice.
4) If the rolled dice number is 1 then the curent score will automatically set to zero and player will be switched.
